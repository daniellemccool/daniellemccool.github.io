Group Maker Privacy Policy

This privacy policy was last modified on 11-06-2020

Group Maker is created by Danielle McCool (d.m.mccool@uu.nl). This page informs you of policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal information related to usage of this bot.

Information Collection 

Group Maker collects no personal information. 

Information Access

Group Maker has access to the Teams manifest of the group to which it is added. Group Maker uses the list of members and associated necessary information for direct messaging but saves no data from this process.
